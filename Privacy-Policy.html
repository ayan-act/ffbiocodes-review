1. Introduction

FF Bio Code respects your privacy and is committed to protecting any personal information you share with us. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have regarding your data.

By using ffbiocodes.com, browsing our pages, or using our Free Fire Bio Code Generator, you agree to the terms outlined in this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Collect

We collect a limited amount of information to operate and improve our website, including:

  • Information you voluntarily provide through our contact forms
  • Your email address, if you choose to submit one
  • Messages or enquiries sent through our contact forms
  • Anonymous analytics data about how visitors use the site
  • Device information, such as device type and screen size
  • Browser type and version
  • Operating system
  • IP address
  • Pages visited and time spent on the website
  • Date and time of each visit
We never collect passwords We never collect Free Fire account credentials

3. How We Use Information

The information we collect is used to:

  • Respond to your questions, messages, and support requests
  • Improve website performance and page loading speed
  • Improve the overall user experience of FF Bio Code
  • Detect, prevent, and reduce spam or abusive activity
  • Analyze traffic patterns to understand how our tools are used
  • Maintain the security and stability of the website

4. Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device by your web browser. They help websites remember your preferences and recognize returning visitors.

FF Bio Code uses cookies to help improve your browsing experience, remember basic preferences, and understand how our website is used. You are free to disable cookies at any time through your browser settings, though doing so may affect certain features of the site.

5. Google AdSense

FF Bio Code uses Google AdSense to display advertisements that help support the website. As part of this service:

  • Google may use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this or other websites
  • Third-party vendors, including Google, use cookies to personalize advertising content
  • Ad personalization may be based on your browsing activity across the web
  • We do not control the cookies placed by Google or other third-party advertising networks

You can manage or opt out of personalized advertising at any time through Google's Ads Settings, available at adssettings.google.com. For more information on how Google uses data from advertising partners, you may also review Google's own privacy practices.

6. Google Analytics

We may use Google Analytics to collect anonymous usage data, such as page views, session duration, and general visitor behavior. This information helps us understand how people use FF Bio Code so we can continue improving our content and tools.

Data collected through Google Analytics is aggregated and anonymous. We do not sell any personally identifiable information to third parties.

7. Third-Party Services

FF Bio Code works with a small number of trusted third-party services to operate the website, including:

  • Google AdSense, for displaying advertisements
  • Google Analytics, for anonymous traffic and usage insights
  • Cloudflare, for website security and performance
  • Our hosting provider, for storing and serving website files

Each of these providers operates under its own privacy policy, and we encourage you to review their terms for a complete understanding of how they handle data.

8. External Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites that are not owned or operated by FF Bio Code. We are not responsible for the privacy practices, content, or terms of any external site. We recommend reviewing the privacy policy of any third-party website you visit.

9. Data Security

We use reasonable administrative and technical measures to help protect the information you share with us. However, no method of transmission over the internet, or method of electronic storage, can be guaranteed to be 100% secure. While we strive to protect your data, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

10. Children's Privacy

FF Bio Code is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we become aware that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will take steps to remove that information from our records.

11. Your Rights

You have certain rights regarding any personal information you have shared with us. You may:

  • Request the deletion of personal information you have submitted to us
  • Contact us with any questions or concerns about how your data is handled
  • Request correction of any inaccurate or outdated information

12. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices, technology, or legal requirements. Any updates will be posted on this page, and the revised version becomes effective immediately upon publication.
